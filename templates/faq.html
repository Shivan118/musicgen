  • How is Music Therapy carried out?

    • Music therapy offers a diverse array of interventions tailored to meet the individual needs and goals of clients. Through passive listening, clients experience relaxation and emotional release, while active engagement in songwriting fosters creativity and self-expression. Whether through therapeutic singing, receptive listening, or guided relaxation, music therapy interventions harness the transformative power of music to promote holistic well-being and enhance quality of life.
